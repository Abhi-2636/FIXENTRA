PRIVACY POLICY

What Fixentra collects and why

Fixentra collects only the information needed to support booking, communication, payments, repeat convenience, and customer support across the service journey.

Profile dataName, phone, email, address
Booking dataService, issue notes, images, timing
Support dataPayment and communication context

What we collect

Account details, saved addresses, booking preferences, service history, issue descriptions, uploaded issue images, payment method choices, and communication metadata may be stored to operate the platform.

How we use the data

Data is used to confirm bookings, match providers, improve service readiness, support customer care, enable repeat booking shortcuts, and maintain records for operational quality and trust.

What we do not claim

Fixentra does not expose unnecessary personal data publicly. Sensitive payment processing is handled through the configured payment flow and is not intentionally surfaced in the customer UI.

User control

Users can update key profile details from account settings. Additional privacy or deletion requests should be routed through the official support channel for review and execution.
