Service, pricing, and booking rules

These terms explain how bookings, price estimates, rescheduling, emergency priority, and service quality support work on Fixentra.

Booking scope

A booking confirms the selected category, locality, date, and time slot. Final work scope may change if on-site inspection reveals additional repair or material requirements not visible at booking time.

Pricing and payments

Fixentra shows price estimates before confirmation whenever possible. Final amounts can change only if the work scope expands or the user approves extra materials or tasks during service.

Cancellation and rescheduling

Users can cancel eligible bookings before completion. Repeated misuse of urgent scheduling, late cancellations, or abusive use of the platform may result in account restrictions.

Service quality promise

The 30-day rework window applies only to supported services and only to the specific work delivered through the booking. It does not cover unrelated future issues or external damage.
