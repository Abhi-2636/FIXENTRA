What we solve

Booking a plumber, electrician, cleaner, or appliance expert should not depend on random negotiation. Fixentra turns that into a guided, screen-friendly workflow that makes the decision faster and clearer.

How the platform works

Users choose a service, locality, preferred visit slot, and issue details. The system then surfaces ETA, expert availability, price context, and repeat-booking shortcuts before confirmation.
